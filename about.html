Our Story

The journey of Mariam Juice Cafe & Dining

A Passion for Quality

Located in the heart of Hailakandi, Mariam Juice Cafe & Dining (MJC) started with a simple vision: to provide a premium, hygienic, and unforgettable culinary experience. What began as a humble juice bar has blossomed into a full-scale dining destination loved by families, professionals, and youth alike.

Our Philosophy

We believe that great food starts with great ingredients. That's why our juices are 100% natural and cold-pressed, our meals are prepared with the freshest produce, and our kitchen adheres to the highest standards of hygiene and safety.

More Than Just Food

MJC is a place to celebrate. Whether it's a cozy family dinner, a lively birthday party, or a quick coffee catch-up, our spaces are designed to make your moments special. We blend elegant ambiance with exceptional service to ensure every visit is memorable.
